Maternity Leave Policy

Eligibility:
Female employees who have completed at least 6 months of service are eligible for maternity leave.

Duration:
Employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per company policy.

Pre and Post Delivery:
Leave can be taken before and after childbirth, as per medical advice.

Benefits:
Full salary is provided during the leave period. Health insurance coverage continues.

Work From Home:
Employees may request work-from-home options after maternity leave based on role and approval.

Rejoining:
Employees are guaranteed the same or equivalent role upon returning.
