Paternity Leave Policy

Eligibility:
Male employees are eligible for paternity leave upon the birth of their child.

Duration:
Employees can avail up to 10 days of paid paternity leave.

Usage:
Leave must be taken within 3 months of the child’s birth.

Benefits:
Full salary is provided during the leave period.

Flexibility:
Additional unpaid leave or work-from-home options may be granted based on manager approval.
